Gaurav Enterprises is a leading engineering company based in Pune, Maharashtra, specializing in CNC wire cutting, EDM, and precision machining since 2007. Our experienced team delivers high-quality dies, jigs, fixtures, and moulds for diverse industries. With a commitment to accuracy, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction, we have built lasting partnerships with major OEMs and SMEs across Maharashtra.

Our Services

CNC Wire Cutting
Precision cutting of complex dies, jigs, fixtures, and moulds with tight tolerances.
EDM Machining
High-accuracy spark erosion for intricate shapes and fine details.
VMC & Milling
Multi-axis machining for components requiring advanced geometry and surface finish.
Grinding & Finishing
Surface and cylindrical grinding for superior accuracy and finish.
Custom Fabrication
Prototyping and small-batch manufacturing for specialized industrial needs.
